   {{ Definition of a *partition* }.def
    **Formal statement**: Let $a,b\in\mathbb R$ with **a<b**.  A *partition* of $[a,b]$ is a finite list of the form *x_0, x_1, ..., x_n*, where

    $$ a = x_0 < x_1 < \dots < x_n = b $$

    **Explanation**: This is meant to represent a sub-division of an interval, where the dividing points are chosen arbitrarily, but there are only finitely many dividing points.
   }

   {{ Definition of the *infimum* and *supremum of a function* }.def
    **Formal statement**: Let **f** be a real-valued function and **A** a subset of the domain of **f**.  Then

    $$\inf_A f = \inf \{ f(x):x\in A\} $$
   }

}
